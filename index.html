Erta Ale

Situated within the remote and inhospitable Danakil Depression, Erta Ale attracts adventurers and volcano enthusiasts from around the world. Its summit crater houses a constantly glowing lava lake, creating a captivating and dramatic spectacle. The volcano's geological significance lies in its location within the East African Rift System, a region characterized by tectonic activity and volcanic eruptions.
